THINGS TO DO IN BARCELONA


We have the perfect plan for your next visit to Barcelona. Come to the Camp Nou for an incomparable experience at one of the most attractive places in the city. You’re not going to miss it, are you?



Buy your official ticket and feel the excitement of FC Barcelona!


If you like good football, there is nothing like seeing a Barça match with your own eyes. Get your seat and join the fans in the stands at the Camp Nou. And don’t hesitate to book the VIP Experience so that you can enjoy the exclusive services available before, during and after the game. Buy your tickets now!



Enjoy the VIP Experience at the Palau Blaugrana


Are you a basketball fan? Come and watch FCB Lassa and feel the magic of the Palau. Buy a VIP ticket and watch the show from inside the arena, just like in the NBA. Or get yourself a Basket Premium package and delight in the exclusive catering service and open bar in the Paris Lounge. Don’t think twice, buy your ticket now!



Take a tour of the Stadium and the Museum with the Camp Nou Experience


There are must-see places in Barcelona: the Sagrada Família, Park Güell and FC Barcelona Stadium. Book the Camp Nou Experience now: it includes a tour of the most famous parts of the Club and a visit to the Barça Museum, where you can relive 120 years of history. So don’t miss it!



Book a table at the Roma 2009 gastronomic venue


Would you like to eat out with views of Barça’s ground? Our gastronomic venue Roma 2009 is the ideal place to enjoy signature cuisine in privileged surroundings: in the upper part of the Camp Nou main stand. Book a table and surprise everyone accompanying you with an exclusive breakfast or lunch.
